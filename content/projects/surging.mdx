Surging Blood is a video game made by myself and 2 friends of mine as part of the final project

This project started at the end of my 3rd year, in October 2024, it is a Fast FPS heavily inspired by [Bioshock](https://store.steampowered.com/app/7670/BioShock/) with a environnement driven story (still is).\
You would unlock a multitude of weapon (sword, revolver, shotgun...) and upgrades for those weapon, some upgrades were inspired by [ULTRAKILL](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1229490/ULTRAKILL/), there was also a speed system where you could slide around on slopes and walls to gain and preserve momentum, but those idea were scrapped to have a more polished game that is reasonable in time

---

You play as Viktor von Faust, a soldier who now works for the Crown, you are sent on an investigation at Cinderval, on the land of Count van Helmont, a brilliant engineer and alchemist who worked on multiple inventions, giving him a lot of appreciation from the Crown, he lives now near a mountain where he founded a mining village to extract a crystal, giving people supernatural powers, but this investigation would turn to be far more complex...

The game is still in developpement, even though we took some time to rest after 2 long years of working on it a lot.
